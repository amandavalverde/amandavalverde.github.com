Al Safar Project

Al Safar Project is a stunning interactive travelogue with rich content and smooth feedback for users to navigate. According to the project Twitter, "Al Safar association follows the legendary 14th century traveler Ibn Battuta to rediscover the territories, cultures and communities on his marvellous journey." This three-year expedition through 38 countries follows the path of medieval explorer Ibn Battuta; while it has been suspended due to travel restrictions as a result of COVID-19, there is an abundance of both video and photographic content displayed for each country through scroll-based animations and transitions. There are also a number of typographic elements tied to those transitions that allow the user to remain fully cognizant of their location on the page. My one major critique for the website is the use of a typeface that is in a handwritten style that is occasionally difficult to read, especially when layered over images.
